Hundru Falls - Malghonghsa

Famous for being one of the highest & most spectacular waterfalls in the Indian state of Jharkhand.

Hundru Falls, located about 45 kilometers from Ranchi, is one of Jharkhand's most famous and spectacular natural attractions. Formed by the Subarnarekha River as it plunges dramatically from a height of 98 meters (320 feet) off the edge of the Chota Nagpur Plateau, it ranks as the 34th highest waterfall in India.

The waterfall is a popular destination for both peaceful picnics and adventure activities. Visitors can enjoy the natural beauty from various viewpoints or make the challenging descent of approximately 750 steps to the base of the falls, where a natural pool offers a place for bathing and relaxation. The area also attracts trekking enthusiasts, and for those seeking an extra thrill, a zip-line facility provides a unique, bird's-eye view across the cascading water.

Highlights:

  • Visitors can enjoy the waterfall, descend to the base and can also swim or bath. Ziplining facility is also available.
  • The rocky terrain and surrounding forest offer opportunities for trekking and hiking enthusiasts.
  • Wear comfortable shoes suitable for trekking, keep the surroundings clean and do not litter.
  • Especially avoid the monsoon season (July to September), as the paths can become slippery & water level is at all time high.
